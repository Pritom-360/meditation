My Story

Hi, I'm Pritom Bhowmik, the creator of MindfulSpace. I'm currently pursuing my undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Engineering at East West University.

MindfulSpace was born from my passion for mindfulness and technology. As a CSE student, I wanted to create a platform that could help others find peace and balance in their daily lives through meditation.

This project combines my technical skills with my personal interest in mental wellness, allowing me to contribute to people's well-being while growing as a developer.

My Values

Accessibility

I believe mental wellbeing tools should be available to all, which is why my core content is free.

Quality

My content is created with care and grounded in mindfulness research.
